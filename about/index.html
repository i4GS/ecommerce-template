Who We Are

We are Malaysia’s homegrown streetwear collective — a hub for fashion-forward individuals who dare to be different. Rooted in Kuala Lumpur’s evolving urban culture, our platform connects the bold energy of the streets with the latest trends in modern apparel. We’re proud to represent and support local and Southeast Asian labels such as UNDG, TNTCO, HYPE, and more — offering a space where authenticity meets style.

Our Mission

We aim to empower a new generation of creators, thinkers, and dreamers through fashion. We’re here to bridge the gap between local talent and the global fashion scene, bringing exclusive, high-quality collections to those who want to make a statement — not just wear clothes.
